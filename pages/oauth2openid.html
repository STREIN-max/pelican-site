An introduction to OAuth 2.0 and OpenID Connect (Tim Speetjens)

In this digital world, interconnected by the web, and with APIs as the way we expose and consume services, OAuth 2.0 and OpenID Connect have gained a lot of popularity.

In this session we will explore the technology behind the "Log in with <insert big name here>". We will have a look at how to securely delegate identity and authorization between multiple parties and explain the multiple pieces like client applications, grants and tokens.

Tim Speetjens

Tim Speetjens is a Freelance Linux System Engineer, with about 20 years of experience. Tim has held a number of different positions, ranging from developer, system engineer, sales engineer and support engineer. Tim focuses on infrastructure and infrastructure automation.
